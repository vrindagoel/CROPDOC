Our Vision

Even though 70 percent of our country’s population in engaged in agriculture yet, the total GDP income of the farming sector is the least of all. Two of the main reasons behind this are disguised unemployment and the lack of information among the farmers regarding how to utilise their land, the weather conditions they are living in and the right kind of fertilisers to use.
They also need to know that despite the traditional crops they are growing , what crops can be grown on the terrain they are living in, which would not only boost their income but also can utilise their land in the most efficient way. Lack of awareness makes the situation even more worse for those living in remote areas . With technology taking lead in almost every segment of the world these days, we, Team Ephiset, aspire to provide these farmers an online portal where in addition to all the perks mentioned above, they can be connected with different agriculture organisations and the government to avail the benefits provided to them.
